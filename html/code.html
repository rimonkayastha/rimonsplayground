GlucoGuide
Hyperglycemia Assistance Application - IBDP Computer Internal Assessment
Flutter, Firebase
The IB Diploma Computer Science Internal Assessment is a core Computer Science assessment requirement where students create an application for a client of their choice. I saw this requirement as an opportunity to help my fupu, my aunt, in my own way. My fupu is diagnosed with hyperglycemia and a part of her treatment process is to record her blood sugar level regularly throughout the day. My app provides a way for her to digitize her blood sugar records and use the information to predict future levels in order to accurately estimate the dose of insulin required to stablize it.
