Rukum Massacre Case
Minidocumentary for a Discrimination Webinar, 9th grade
In 9th grade, we students conducted an online webinar to raise awareness about discrimination. I chose to present a real-life case documentary to highlight the impact of caste-based prejudice, still prevalent in rural Nepal. Through my voice, I aimed to evoke a feeling of urgency among the audience to prevent such tragedies from happening again.

Kala Yatra
Advertisement Video, 2023
Kala Yatra was an art exhibition hosted in the Academy of Fine Arts, Nepal, where underrepresented artists promoting local and traditional artforms were given a stage to present their artworks. As the IT Officer of the Interact Club of Kathmandu, I also developed this advertisement to digitally market it through social media and maximize the attendee turnout.
